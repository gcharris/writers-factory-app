The guard leaned forward to inspect the neatly stacked laundry. In that moment of focused attention, the driver struck the base of his neck. The guard collapsed without sound, caught before hitting the ground.

With methodical movements, the driver deposited the unconscious body in the van among the laundry bags. He took the guard's magnetic key, walking purposefully to the security post.

The electronic lock yielded with a soft CLICK. The driver dropped the key through the window before returning to the van.

He pulled through the open gate, accelerating gently onto the access road. The rearview mirror showed no pursuit, only the facility's lights gradually diminishing behind them.

"No one following," he noted to his passenger, who had rematerialized beside him.

At the security post, the passenger appeared from the shadows. He stripped off his white coveralls, revealing a guard uniform underneath that matched the unconscious man's attire perfectly.

He entered the small booth and began typing. The security logs disappeared from the screen erasing all evidence of the van's passage. Timestamps, gate records, and camera footage vanished with a few keystrokes.

He picked up the magnetic key and put it around his neck. His posture shifted subtly, adopting the alert but relaxed stance of regular security personnel.
