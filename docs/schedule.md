# Weekly Schedule

**Course Code:** ISP-2026 | **Format:** 5-Day Intensive | **Platform:** Writers Factory Desktop

---

## Day 1: The Machine & The Voice

### Morning Session
- Installation Verification
- Multi-Agent Tour: Meet the Foreman, understand the four modes
- System Health Check: Verify all components running

### Afternoon Session
- **Voice Extraction Party**
- Feed NotebookLM your personal writing samples
- Generate initial "Starter Skills" voice profile
- First dialogue with your calibrated AI partner

### Homework
Generate 1,000 words using your voice profile. Compare to your natural writing.

---

## Day 2: Narrative Architecture

### Focus
Story as System Design - treating narrative elements as interconnected data

### Activities
- Define your Story Spec: Logline, Target Audience, Tone
- Build your Protagonist profile: Fatal Flaw, The Lie, Arc trajectory
- Map character relationships in the Knowledge Graph

### Technical Deep Dive
Using GraphRAG to model character contradictions and thematic resonance

### Deliverable
Complete 15-beat outline loaded into the Knowledge Graph

---

## Day 3: The Drafting Pipeline

### Focus
High-velocity generation with quality controls

### Activities
- Enter **Director Mode** for the first time
- Run Tournament Mode: Generate 3 variations of opening scene
- Learn to evaluate and select the strongest version
- Practice iterative refinement workflow

### Milestone
At 2,500 words generated, your skills upgrade from "Starter" to "Novel-Tuned"

### Goal
50% of draft complete by end of day

---

## Day 4: Diagnostics & Polish

### Focus
Debugging the Narrative - using analytical tools to find weaknesses

### Activities
Using the Narrative Dashboard:
- **Tension Indicator**: Analyze conflict pacing across scenes
- **Pacing Analysis**: Balance action sequences vs. setup scenes
- **Community Detection**: Identify character clusters and subplots

### Tracks Split

**Writer Track:**
- Polish a 3-page excerpt for public reading
- Focus on voice consistency and emotional impact

**Architect Track:**
- Draft technical proposal for system improvement
- Identify specific bottleneck or enhancement opportunity

---

## Day 5: Showcase

### Morning
- Final manuscript assembly
- Last-minute polish and continuity checks
- [Submit Your Work](/portal/submit)

### Afternoon: Presentations (10 mins each)

**Creative Presentations (Writer Track):**
- Share your premise and logline
- Read your polished 3-page excerpt
- Discuss your experience with AI collaboration

**Technical Presentations (Architect Track):**
- Present the problem you identified
- Explain your proposed solution
- Demonstrate impact with metrics or examples
