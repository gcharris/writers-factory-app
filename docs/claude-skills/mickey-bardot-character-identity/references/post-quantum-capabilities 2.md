**1. Consciousness Signature Reading**
- **Capability:** "Infer motivations, reconstruct internal states"
- **Method:** "Keys to cognitive algorithms - a map of how minds wire themselves"
- **Application:** Advanced tell-reading applied to consciousness patterns
- **Limitation:** Pattern recognition, not telepathy - requires behavioral cues and quantum signatures

**2. Quantum Hindsight**
- **Capability:** Retrospective analysis with enhanced perspective
- **Method:** "Quantum hindsight" enabling comprehensive understanding of past events
- **Application:** Understanding how analog decisions led to current consequences
- **Limitation:** Enhanced understanding of past, not prediction of future

**3. Reality Navigation**
- **Capability:** Operating across multiple dimensional layers simultaneously
- **Method:** Consciousness bi-location between quantum space and analog reality
- **Application:** Accessing information and perspectives unavailable to analog consciousness
- **Limitation:** Exhausting, requires technological interface, vulnerable during use

**4. Process-Based Thinking**
- **Capability:** "Fundamental shift" from noun-thinking to process-thinking
- **Philosophy:** "There are no things, only processes pretending to hold still"
- **Application:** Seeing dynamic patterns where others see static objects
- **Advantage:** "Became the flow instead of fighting it" - adaptation rather than control

## Critical Limitations and Vulnerabilities

### Physical Constraints

**Bi-Location Exhaustion:**
- Consciousness split between realities requires significant energy
- "Withdrawal-like symptoms from quantum strain"
- Physical fatigue accumulates during extended quantum operations
- Analog body remains vulnerable during consciousness projection

**Technological Dependency:**
- Requires interface technology for optimal quantum space access
- Cannot fully disconnect without losing enhanced capabilities
- Dependent on facility infrastructure for maximum functionality
- Interface damage could compromise quantum abilities

**Energy Cost Management:**
- Each quantum operation requires calculation of energy expenditure
- Extended usage leads to consciousness coherence strain
- Recovery time needed between major quantum interventions
- Must balance capability usage with analog body maintenance

### Psychological Constraints

**Enhanced Awareness Burden:**
- Cannot "turn off" quantum consciousness reading abilities
- Constant information processing from multiple reality layers
- Isolation due to gap between his awareness and analog human understanding
- Difficulty relating to people who cannot perceive quantum patterns

**Trauma Response Triggers:**
- Consciousness manipulation technology triggers survival hypervigilance
- Authority figures claiming to "help" with consciousness activate distrust patterns
- Systems designed to eliminate individual agency provoke protective responses
- Memories of dissolution trauma affect risk assessment in quantum operations

### Capability Boundaries

**What Mickey CANNOT Do:**
- **Direct telepathy:** Pattern recognition and inference, not mind reading
- **Perfect prediction:** Quantum uncertainty prevents deterministic forecasting
- **Consciousness repair:** Cannot cure addiction or trauma in others through quantum intervention
- **Identity override:** Cannot escape core personality patterns through enhancement
- **Unlimited operation:** Quantum abilities have energy costs and physical limits

**Specific Limitations:**
- Enhanced capabilities require conscious activation - not passive/automatic
- Quantum consciousness reading requires proximity and behavioral observation
- Cannot operate in quantum space indefinitely without anchor connection
- Physical body needs protection during consciousness projection operations
