## Pre-Course Setup
**Status:** Mandatory
**Deadline:** 48 hours before Day 1

1.  **Install the Software:** Use the link above for your track.
2.  **The "Health Check":** Open the app. If you see the "Welcome to Writers Factory" screen, you are ready.
3.  **NotebookLM Preparation:** You must create a NotebookLM account and upload 5,000 words of *personal writing* (emails, journals, blogs) to train your Voice Avatar.

## Weekly Schedule

### **Day 1: The Machine & The Voice**
* **Morning:** Installation Verification & Multi-Agent Tour.
* **Afternoon:** **Voice Extraction Party.** We will feed your NotebookLM data into the Factory to generate your custom "Starter Skills".
* **Homework:** Generate your first 1,000 words using your specific voice profile.

### **Day 2: Narrative Architecture**
* **Focus:** Story as System Design.
* **Activity:** Defining the "Story Spec" (Logline, Audience, Tone).
* **Tech:** Using the Graph to model character internal contradictions and state.
* **Deliverable:** A 15-beat outline committed to the Knowledge Graph.

### **Day 3: The Drafting Pipeline**
* **Focus:** High-velocity generation.
* **Activity:** Running "Tournament Mode" to generate 5 variations of key scenes.
* **The Upgrade Moment:** Once you hit 2,500 words, the system upgrades your skills from "Starter" to "Novel-Tuned".
* **Goal:** 50% of draft complete.

### **Day 4: Diagnostics & Polish**
* **Focus:** Debugging the Narrative.
* **Activity:** Using "Pacing Graphs" to find flat spots in the tension arc.
* **Tracks Split:**
    * *Writers:* Polish a 3-page excerpt for reading.
    * *Engineers:* Draft a technical proposal for a system improvement (e.g., "Better Graph Pruning").

### **Day 5: Showcase**
* **Morning:** Final assembly.
* **Afternoon:** Presentations (10 mins).
    * *Creative:* Premise + Reading.
    * *Technical:* Problem + Solution + Impact.
