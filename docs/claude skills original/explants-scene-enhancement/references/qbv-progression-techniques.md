## The QBV Foundation

**Core Principle**: Mickey's voice evolution reflects **quantum capability emergence**, not external contamination.

**The QBV Progression**:
1. **Phase 1: Edge Bleeding** - Pre-existing quantum sensitivity manifesting
2. **Phase 2: Intensification** - Capabilities becoming harder to dismiss
3. **Phase 3: Takeover** - Quantum awareness becoming primary reality
4. **Phase 4: Integration** - Quantum capabilities as new baseline

**Why This Matters**: Mickey survives transformation because QBVs trained his consciousness for quantum operation over 40+ years. Voice evolution reflects this **earned capability development**, not random corruption.
